## 📋 What is captions?

captions is a simple tool that turns live speech on TikTok streams into text. It shows real-time captions on your screen while you watch TikTok LIVE videos. It also supports multiple languages by translating what is spoken. This can help people follow conversations better, especially if they have hearing difficulties or speak different languages.

This app works on Windows and does not require technical skills to run. You can watch TikTok LIVE streams with subtitles right away.

## ❓ How captions works

captions uses advanced speech-to-text technology to listen to TikTok LIVE audio. It converts spoken words into text quickly and accurately. The app connects directly to the TikTok API to get real-time audio data. Then, it processes this data in the background and shows subtitles on the screen.

The translation feature uses language models to provide live text conversion from the original language into your choice of another one. This helps viewers understand streams in foreign languages.
